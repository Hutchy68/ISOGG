Meetings and Events

For all our upcoming meetings and events please see our calendar below. You can also open our calendar in a new browser window. The ISOGG meeting schedule is tentative and subject to change. Non-ISOGG genetic genealogy meetings may be listed, but MUST be open to the public and the presenter an ISOGG member.

Please email if you're interested attending a meeting/event listed or adding a meeting/event to our calendar.

Our Calendar

Access our calendar in other applications like Microsoft Outlook or Apple Calendar using the Subscribe link below.

NOTE - All Events will be shown in UTC time. ISOGG members are located around the world as are events. If you use the 'Subscribe' link below or click on the event title and save the event to your personal calendar the UTC time will become your local time zone.

Regional Coordinators

ISOGG meetings are coordinated by volunteer Regional Coordinators (RC). An ISOGG meeting may be combined with a genetic genealogy workshop that's open to the public.
